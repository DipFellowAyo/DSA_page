Sales Agent - Dukka

Dukka is building a smart and easy-to-use bookkeeping application suite of business tools for micro-small african merchants.

We strongly believe that everyone should be able to participate and thrive in today's digital economy, and no one should be left out because the technology is too great or the cost is too high. If you are someone who thrives in fast-paced innovative environments and is organized, communicates well, and practices excellent time management skills, then the role of a Dukka Sales' Agent at Dukka Inc. might be right for you.
